# Risk Assistant Demo

## Product

A mobile, AI-native risk-control assistant for executives and risk teams. The product turns risk signals into an auditable management loop: discover, assess, explain, decide, assign, track, and report.

## Users

- Executives scan material changes, impacts, decisions, and overdue actions.
- Risk leaders prioritize, escalate, assign, and supervise.
- Risk specialists verify evidence and execute follow-up.
- Risk managers prepare monitoring and reporting material.

## Experience Principles

- Mobile executive scanning first, around a 390 px viewport.
- Warm cream surfaces, restrained orange emphasis, generous rounded cards, and low-contrast shadows.
- Every page has a clear decision and next action; not every page is a dashboard.
- AI must expose evidence, data time, scope, assumptions, uncertainty, and a human confirmation point.
- Risk level, event trend, verification, tracking, and report status remain separate concepts.

## Investment Risk Contract

The investment-risk module is driven by one reviewed monthly snapshot. Overview, changes, VaR, performance, structure, member and asset detail, Copilot, tracking, and report views all derive their numbers and evidence from that snapshot. The module provides management analysis, not investment or trading advice.

## Accessibility and Motion

Use semantic controls, visible focus, 44 px tap targets, keyboard-operable dialogs, readable contrast, and reduced-motion fallbacks. Charts always include text equivalents and must not use color as their only signal.
